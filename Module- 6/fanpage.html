Slip Knot Tribute Page

Slipknot is a heavy metal band from Des Moines, Iowa. After several lineup changes in its early years, the band settled on nine members for more than a decade. Slipknot is well known for its attention-grabbing image, aggressive style of music, and energetic and chaotic live shows.
